YANTHRIKA EVENT #4

Kalopsia - Get Your Squad. Play. Win

Date : February 07, 2019

Prize Worth : 4k

A team based three round event challenging the engineering, problem solving skills and communication skills of players.

Round 1: Quiz
Will involve application level questions in which the students are supposed to find answers . Using internet for reference is allowed.

Level 2: PUBG
A series of team versus team PUBG battles where the winning teams advance into the final round

Level 3: Circuit Building Contest
A spontaneous circuit building contest where students will be asked to build a circuit based on the output provided.

Rules and Regulations

Each team must consist a minimum of 2 or a maximum 5 members.
Team players won't be allowed to talk to each other during the event. Writing or texting inorder to communicate is allowed. You can use internet in order to solve problems.
The qualification of team to the next round will be solely based on the degree of completion and time taken to do the same.
There decision of the Co-ordinators in charge will be final.
Event will start at 10:00am. Any team that fails to be present at the venue for a round will be automatically disqualified.

elixir

YANTHRIKA EVENT #5

Elixir - Proteus Circuit Designing

Date : February 07, 2019

Prize Worth : 4k

It's a circuit designing in proteus. We are providing a circuit diagram and the candidates should draw it in proteus within time limit.
One who completes it in minimum time gets the prize.

It's a technical fun game event which is having different levels. One who completes each level will get a present. Levels are divided as follows:

Level 1: Puzzle Solving
A small puzzle is given to the candidate,he/she must complete it in least time.

Level 2: LED Blinking
A small circuit with incomplete connection will be provided, one should complete the circuit and light the led. There will be time limit. (Note: Circuit will be there for cross-checking)

Level 3: Snake Xenzia
Score maximum points within time limit. There will be keys for moving the snake.

Level 4: Led Colour Matching
In short span of time participant should blink the exact colour led that has been requested. There will be rotating nobe which helps to blink the exact led.

Level 5: BullsEye
Its a game played using darts. One who hits close to the bullseye wins the game. (Note: limited number of arrows can be thrown)

Level 6: Ring Toss
Participant throw rings to a fixed target for limited chances wins the game.

Level 7: Balloons Cracking
Participants are provided with a gun. Using this gun he/she should crack the balloon. There will be limited amount of pellets. One who cracks maximum number of balloons will be the winner.

Note: One who completes ELIXIR can join into ESPERO. If he/she is unable to do ELIXIR they can directly enter into ESPERO
